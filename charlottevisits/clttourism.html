Best tourists spots in Charlotte, North Carolina

If you are looking for some places to visit in the Charlotte area this page is exactly what you were looking for. As I go ahead and break down the top tourist locations and places to visit.


#1 South Park Mall

Southpark Mall located in the Southpark neighborhood in Charlotte is an upscale mall notable for having high-end designer outlets such as Louis Vuitton, Neiman Marcus, Burberry, Lacoste, and the list goes on. By the way, you won't find another Neiman Marcus anywhere in the Carolinas as South Park is home to the one and only. With over 177 stores and is the largest by square footage on the east coast of the United States. It's a must to visit there if you are visiting Charlotte and it will not disappoint.

#2 Noda

Noda, short for North Davidson, is known as Charlotte's arts center. Located about a mile away from the downtown area Noda offers a unique atmosphere with colorful graffiti, some places to get a bite to eat at, and fun little gift shops all within walking distance. As Noda is only one square mile big it's easy to get from one side to the other pretty quick.



#3 Freedom Park

Want to get away from the city for a minute and spend some quality time outside? Freedom Park is located in the middle of a neighborhood right outside of the downtown area and is known as one of the best parks in Charlotte. Offering great outdoor activities whether you want to play basketball or soccer. Go fly fishing, or take a hike down the scenic nature trails. This place has a little something for everyone. Even though this park is located close to the city there is a lot of room there and is a pretty huge park with having about 98 acres of land.



#4 Lazy 5 Ranch

Lazy 5 Ranch is a unique experience that is hard to find anywhere else. Either you can take a ride on a carriage or if you want to take the risk you can drive your car through this ranch located in Mooresville about 30 minutes away from the downtown area. You can feed live animals whether it's a zebra, buffalo, or even an ostrich. As you guide yourself through the trail of unique live animals that you get to feed they provide some food for you too. The best part is they let you go through the trail as many times as you want. So go wild and be an animal!!!



#5 Charlotte Sports

A lot of people who follow football will know immediately who the Carolina Panthers are and what Bank of America stadium is. But that's not the only sport Charlotte has in town. There is also an NBA team with the Charlotte Hornets that play at the spectrum center. Or maybe you want to eat a hotdog, grab a baseball glove, and watch a baseball game at Trust Field with the Charlotte Knights. But wait there's more, how about hockey? Yes, Charlotte even has a hockey team that alternates playing games at the Spectrum Center along with the Hornets. If you are a sports fan Charlotte is a great city with many great sports teams and the venues will not disappoint. In particular, the Knights Stadium is the newest of them all with a gorgeous field that has the city skyline painted in the background.



#6 White Water Raffting Center

The White Water Rafting Center is not just about getting in a boat pumped up with air and then go crashing into some hard waves (Although it's very fun and you can do that). The White Water Center provides you with more than that by having obstacle courses across rope ladders and swings that are 20 feet in the air. They also have zip lines, mountain biking trails with bike rentals, and even rock climbing walls. A lot of people come and visit the White Water Center without even stepping foot in a raft because of all the other things they have to do there.



#7 NASCAR Hall of Fame

The Nascar Hall of Fame in Charlotte is a very fun experience even if you don't know that much about NASCAR or even racing for that matter. This place shows you first hand the history of NASCAR and lets you get a feel for what NASCAR is like up and close. They offer to let you go around the track either with a group of people or by yourself and it's very cool to experience the angles and degrees they race at.



#8 Wings Haven

For the die hard nature lovers out there Wing Haven is a spot you will fall in love with. Located right in the heart of Charlotte this place has a set of two large gardens that you can go and explore. With lots of wildlife, mainly being birds, this spot is great for the people that love gardening or birdwatching as there are hundreds of birds on the property providing them with habitat.



#9 Carolina Raptor Center

The Carolina Raptor center home to many.. raptors is located just North of Charlotte at the Latta Plantation. Here you can come to visit the wide variety of eagles, hawks, vultures, owls, and falcons all of these birds are also known as raptors. with 57 acres of land to preserve these birds, you will get some educational displays on how these birds behave in their environment. The Raptor center is home to 23 different species of raptors and you will get to see them on display.



#10 Carowinds

One of the main attractions that Charlotte has to offer is the amusement park that is located right on the border of North and South Carolina called Carowinds. A very well-known amusement park with crazy fast rollercoasters such as the Fury 325 or the newest to the collection of thrilling rides Copperhead Strike. Carowinds is home to the two largest steel roller coasters in North America and is ranked fifth for having the most roller coasters in one park. Carowinds is an experience that you would get nowhere else and is why it must-do if you live in the Charlotte area.



#11 Epicentre

Now for last but not least, we have the Epicentre. The Epicentre is a perfect spot to meet up with friends, grab a bite to eat, or go see its outdoor mall. They also do live music during the summer called "Live After Five" and a lot of people come and have fun and get drinks while enjoying the music. There are also a couple of nightclubs for people wanting to learn about the nightlife in Charlotte. There is also a bowling alley called strike city and a movie theater that serves drinks. There is also a rooftop venue with skyline views that also host concerts. You could not go wrong with coming here as there is so much to do and so much to see it's hardly ever boring there.

To Conclude

This list is not in any order it's just a list that has a little something for everyone to try if it's exploring the city, experiencing nature, or going to an amusement park there are countless things to do for all types of people.
